Common questions

  • Does the app work offline? Yes. Your lists and notes are stored locally on your device.
  • When is an internet connection required? Only when you use optional features such as AI (and voice recognition may require internet depending on your device/provider settings).
  • AI is not working Check connection, update the app, and retry. If the issue persists, contact support and include device model + Android version.
  • Backup / restore If you use export/import, ensure the backup file is accessible and you have storage permissions where applicable.

Bug reports

When reporting a bug, include:
• App version
• Device model + Android version
• What you expected vs. what happened
• Steps to reproduce (if possible)
