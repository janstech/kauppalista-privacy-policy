Delete your data

The app stores shopping lists and notes locally on your device. To delete all app data:

Android (general steps)

  1. Open Settings
  2. Go to Apps → Shopping List & Notes
  3. Open Storage
  4. Tap Clear data (and optionally Clear cache)
  5. Optionally uninstall the app to remove it completely

If you use export/import backups, deleting app data does not automatically delete files you exported elsewhere. Delete those files manually from your storage if needed.
